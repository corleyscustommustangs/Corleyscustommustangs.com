Mustang-Focused Performance

Corleys Custom Mustangs is based in Afton, Oklahoma and specializes exclusively in Mustang performance, upgrades, and custom builds.

From camshaft installations and timing services to suspension, drivetrain work, and full builds, every project is handled with attention to detail and a performance-first mindset.

We believe in doing the job right — no shortcuts, no rushed work. Whether you're upgrading a street car or planning a full build, your Mustang is treated like it’s our own.
